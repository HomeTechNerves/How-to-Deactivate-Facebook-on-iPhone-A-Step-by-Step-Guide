Deactivating your Facebook account on iPhone can be a great way to take a break from social media while keeping your account intact. If you're wondering [how to deactivate Facebook on iPhone](https://hometechnerves.com/how-to-deactivate-facebook-on-iphone/), follow these simple steps to temporarily deactivate your profile.

Open the Facebook App
Launch the Facebook app on your iPhone and log in to your account.

Access Settings
Tap the three horizontal lines (menu) at the bottom-right corner. Scroll down and select Settings & Privacy, then tap Settings.

Find Your Facebook Information
Scroll to Your Facebook Information and tap Account Ownership and Control.

Deactivate Your Account
Select Deactivation and Deletion. Choose Deactivate Account and tap Continue to Account Deactivation.

Confirm Deactivation
Follow the prompts, select the reason for deactivation, and hit Deactivate. Enter your password to confirm.

By deactivating your Facebook account, your profile and posts will be hidden, but your data will remain intact. You can easily reactivate it whenever you're ready.
